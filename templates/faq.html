Our eco-friendly tableware products are made from naturally fallen palm leaves and sugarcane bagasse, which is the fibrous residue remaining after sugarcane stalks are crushed to extract their juice.

Yes, all of our palm leaf and sugarcane products are 100% biodegradable and compostable, making them an excellent choice for environmentally conscious consumers.

Absolutely. Our palm leaf plates are made without any chemicals or additives, making them safe for serving all types of food.

Yes, our tableware is designed to handle both hot and cold foods. Palm leaf and sugarcane products are naturally heat-resistant and can safely hold hot meals, as well as cold dishes.

Palm leaf products can withstand temperatures up to 350°F (177°C) for up to 45 minutes, making them safe for use in the microwave and oven. However, it's best to avoid prolonged exposure to high heat. Sugarcane products are microwave-safe but not recommended for oven use.

Our tableware items are sturdy and durable, capable of holding heavy and moist foods without bending or leaking. Palm leaf plates, in particular, are known for their strength and resilience.

While our products are designed for single use to ensure hygiene and convenience, palm leaf plates can be gently hand-washed and reused a few times if handled with care. Sugarcane products are intended for single use only.

Simply dispose of our tableware products in your compost bin. They will naturally break down and return to the earth without leaving any toxic residues.

Yes, our products are perfect for outdoor events such as picnics, barbecues, and weddings. They are sturdy enough to handle outdoor conditions and biodegradable, making cleanup easy and eco-friendly.

Yes, we offer bulk purchasing options for events, businesses, and caterers. Please contact us directly for more information and to receive a customized quote.

Our palm leaf products are sourced from sustainable plantations in India, where naturally fallen leaves are collected and processed. Our sugarcane products are made from bagasse, a byproduct of sugar production, ensuring minimal waste.

We use eco-friendly packaging materials to minimize our environmental impact. Our products are packaged in recyclable or compostable materials.

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on the destination. Please check our shipping policy for more details.

Yes, we offer customization options for bulk orders. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs and to receive a quote for custom printing.

Our tableware is made from renewable, sustainable materials that decompose naturally and quickly, unlike plastic or Styrofoam, which can take hundreds of years to break down and release harmful chemicals into the environment.
